ABOUT ME

Welcome to Elias Chang's personal space Station!

The ancients said that a good person does not have only one skill. Becoming a polymath like Da Vinci is a formidable challenge, yet I'm committed to continuously refining my skills and expanding my knowledge.

Human knowledge, accumulated over millennia, resembles a mountain whose peak is beyond sight and an ocean whose depths are unfathomable, it is so broad and profound that even if a person spends his entire life studying it, he would still only know a fraction of it. So for me, the road to learning is still long and difficult.

Nevertheless, my passion for learning burns fiercely, and my resolve to make progress remains unyielding; this journey is so far away, but I'm still willing to walk it. Here, I aim to refine my understanding and share insights that may inspire and assist you. For any lapses in rigor, I seek your understanding and forgiveness.

“Be broadly learned, inquire thoroughly, reflect carefully, discern clearly, and practice earnestly.” — The Book of Rites
